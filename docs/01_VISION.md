# ScholarVerse Vision

ScholarVerse is a gamified learning ecosystem designed for college students.

## Mission

Help students become consistent learners through
gamification, competition and community.

## Core Loop

Study
↓

Complete Tasks
↓

Earn XP
↓

Level Up
↓

Unlock Rewards
↓

Compete on Leaderboards
↓

Achieve Career Goals

## Long-Term Goal

Become the world's best student productivity platform.
